Before you write

A few things that may answer your question faster:

  • Does Subnix collect my data? No. Subnix has no accounts, analytics, or tracking — see the Privacy Policy.
  • Ping shows 100% loss. ICMP is restricted in some sandboxes; Subnix defaults to a TCP connect mode. Use the TCP option, or the “Retry with TCP” prompt.
  • LAN Scanner or Bonjour finds nothing. Grant Local Network access when prompted, and make sure you're on Wi-Fi rather than cellular.

Bug reports & feature requests

You're also welcome to open an issue on GitHub. Please include your device, OS version, and the tool involved.
